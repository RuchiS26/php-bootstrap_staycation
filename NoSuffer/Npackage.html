National Packages


Rajasthan

The land of royalty, Rajasthan with its rich and vibrant life, and culture will ooze out all the distinct colors that make up the kind and generous people of this unique state. Its palaces and forts are reminders of the many kingdoms that historically vied for the region and a single visit to the different monumental sights that are offered by this state, will leave you anxious for your next trip here.

Shimla

Situated at the edge of the mighty Himalayas, this hill station is home to one of the largest natural ice skating rink in South Asia. Shimla is also the largest city and the capital of the state of Himachal Pradesh. Other Attractions including the Viceroy Lodge, the Christ Church, the Jakhoo Temple, the Mall Road and the Ridge, make this city a top tourist location that everyone should visit at least once in their life.

God’s own country, Kerala has a lot to offer to everyone that visits this state. With its rich backwaters and beautiful culture, it should definitely be on everybody’s travel list.

Goa

With beautiful white sand beaches and a fun filled active night life, it’s no wonder that this tourist destination is the most popular among young adults and students.


Hampi

Once the capital of the many dynasties, this historical location houses many famous monuments and building such as the thousand pillar temple and the famous ‘chakra’ temple.

Delhi

The capital of India, Delhi also forms the largest union territory of the country. Previously used as the capital for the Mughal empire, the city is filled with historical monuments and it is also a few hours away from the famous Taj Mahal.


Tirupati

This pilgrimage site is one of the most visited temples in the world, with about half a million people visiting this shrine every single day to pay their respects to Lord Venkateswara. Tirupati is also synonymous with the famous ‘seven hills’ that the pilgrims need to cross before reaching the temple.

Mumbai

The financial capital of the country, Mumbai is also the most populated city in India. Nobody knows till date, the exact precise number of people that live and currently inhibit this city.
